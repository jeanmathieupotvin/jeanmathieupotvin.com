fs :: flood simulators

Large scale simulations of storm surges and fluvial floods.

Flood Simulators is a collection of models aiming to simulate yearly losses stemming from fluvial and coastal floods. The goal was to create a robust and reliable tool that could be used to assess flood risk at each exposure's location without relying on past claim amounts. An ordered chain of multiple advanced probabilistic and physics models is used instead. This work generalizes the framework proposed by Boudreault et al. (2019) by its extents and by allowing countrywide spatial dependence between the exposures.

Internal private project initiated for The Co-operator's General Insurance Company (CGIC). 2018 - 2020.
